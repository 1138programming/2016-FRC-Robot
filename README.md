## Talon and Solenoid Definitons:

### Talons:
- 1 - Front Base Right
- 2 - Front Base Left
- 3 - Rear Base Right
- 4 - Rear Base Left
- 5 - Flywheel Right
- 6 - Flywheel Left
- 7 - Filter Right
- 8 - Filter Left
- 9 - Collector
- 10 - Spare

### Solenoids:
- 0 and 1 - Base Solenoid (shift)
- 2 and 3 - Collector/Ratchet Solenoid
- 4 and 5 - Esophagus
